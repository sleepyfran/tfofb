About the project

Twisted Face Of Fading Beauty is the artistic name of Fran González, a project that started back in 2013 as a way to experiment with every genre he listens to.

News

22 December 2017

After more than two years in the making my new album Take Care is almost ready. It'll be available in January 1, 2018. In the meantime you can check out the amazing cover that my great friend and amazing photographer Paola Paulino made in the music section.

You can also listen to the first released track of the album, How Long Was Forever Again? right now on Bandcamp
