About the project

Twisted Face Of Fading Beauty is the artist name of Fran González, taken from the lyrics of Looking Outside Inside by Anathema, who has always been a great inspiration for me.

I started out this project in 2013 and since then, I've released two EP's and one LP that you can check out in the Music section.

Also, you can contact me if you want to say anything in the Contact section.
